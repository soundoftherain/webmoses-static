Mission

Our mission is to decentralize opportunity. By blending proof-of-work and proof-of-quality, WebMoses ensures every builder, artist, and thinker can earn and grow through open collaboration.

How It Works

1. Post a Project

Creators list open tasks and define tokenized reward structures.

2. Build & Earn

Contributors complete tasks, get verified on-chain, and earn rewards.

3. Govern Together

Ownership is shared via DAO — decisions, rewards, and growth.
