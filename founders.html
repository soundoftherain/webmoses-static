Meet the Founders

The vision behind WebMoses is to empower creators and builders through a network that values proof of quality over profit.

The Origin

WebMoses was born from a simple realization — the future of work isn’t managed by platforms, but by people. Decentralized collaboration allows creators to earn from verified contributions and share ownership transparently.

By combining blockchain incentives with real creative output, we’re building a protocol that turns work itself into an investable, community-owned asset.

Founder’s Statement

“I’ve seen too many great builders work in silence, with no visibility or reward. WebMoses changes that. Our mission is to prove that decentralized work can empower individuals — not exploit them.”

— Moses Builder, Founder
