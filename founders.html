Moses Builder — Founder

Hello, I’m Moses Builder. I envision a platform that changes how programmers view work — a place where projects are tokenized, contributors are rewarded for quality, and governance emerges through participation.

Mission

Create a decentralized workflow where project stakes, proof-of-work, and proof-of-quality combine to surface top contributors and fairly distribute rewards in ETH or project tokens.

What We Need

  • Core contributors: Solidity developers, backend, frontend.
  • Early adopters and pilot projects to test reward flows.
  • Minimal funding to bootstrap audits and infrastructure.
